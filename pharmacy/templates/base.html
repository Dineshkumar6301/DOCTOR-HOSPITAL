Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

How do I book a consultation? +
Just open the app, search for a doctor by specialty or health issue, choose your preferred time slot, and confirm the booking. It only takes a few clicks!
Are the doctors on Docpro verified? +
Yes, all doctors on our platform are fully certified, licensed, and verified by our medical team before being listed for consultations.
How much does a consultation cost? +
Consultation fees vary depending on the doctor and specialty. You’ll see the fee before confirming the appointment — no hidden charges.
Is my consultation private and secure? +
Absolutely. Your health data and conversations are encrypted and 100% confidential. We strictly follow data privacy guidelines to protect your information.
Can I consult for my family members too? +
Yes, you can create profiles for your parents, children, or spouse under one account and book consultations for them easily.
Do I get a prescription after the consultation? +
Yes, if the doctor finds it necessary, you'll receive a digital prescription right after your consultation, which you can download or show at your local pharmacy.
